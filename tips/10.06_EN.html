Encryption as insurance against data loss

What really protects information when a device is lost

Hello everyone,

When we work outside the office, travel or work remotely, the risk of losing or having a laptop, external drive or corporate device stolen is real. In these situations, protecting information does not only depend on recovering the device, but on preventing third parties from accessing the data stored on it.

Encryption turns the information on a device into unreadable data for anyone who does not have the right key. This means that if the device is lost or stolen, the impact may be limited to the physical equipment and not become a corporate data leak.

Losing a device should not mean losing the data.

Encryption is a key measure for protecting laptops and devices that leave the office, especially for roles that travel, work externally or handle sensitive information.

 

Protection against physical loss

If an encrypted laptop is lost or stolen, an unauthorised person should not be able to access the stored files, emails or documents without the corresponding key.
 

Locked data

Encryption protects information even if someone removes the hard drive and tries to read it from another computer. A login password alone does not provide the same level of protection.
 

An increasingly common measure

Encryption is being progressively implemented in many organisations, prioritising devices that move frequently or contain confidential information.
 

Immediate reporting

Even if a device is encrypted, any loss or theft must be reported immediately so that security protocols can be activated, access reviewed and additional risks reduced.

Recommendation

Check that corporate devices used outside the office have encryption enabled. Do not rely only on the user login password: disk encryption is the barrier that protects data if the device falls into the wrong hands. If you lose a laptop, external drive or USB device, report it immediately through the established channels, even if you believe it did not contain important information.
